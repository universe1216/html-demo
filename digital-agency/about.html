ABOUT

Relentless creativity.
Fueled by data.

Digital has not only created new opportunities for brands but also more accountability for the agencies they work with. Gone are the days of prestige campaigns that win awards but not market share. Now, it’s about the results you can measure, the revenue you can trace back to strategic touchpoints. It’s about an agency devoting itself to top tier creative work and leveraging data to drive results.

Union is an independent creative & performance marketing agency.

Our position is simple – every client is our partner and we are invested in the success of every campaign. Specializing in travel & tourism, health & wellness, and food & beverage brands, we bring new ideas to life in digital campaigns that grow your business. It’s what our partners expect. And it’s why our work matters.

BENEFITS

Obsessed with data-driven performance.

Union is a team of experts focused on data & brand strategy, performance media and branded content. At a time when more consumers are looking to buy directly from brands, we help brands capitalize on this direct-to-consumer paradigm shift and find success in increasingly competitive markets. We believe every marketing dollar must be viewed as an investment and we prove the ROI of our work with quantifiable data.

About the Ageny

  • Drive Revenue

    While impressions, clicks and engagement are important metrics, success is defined by revenue. Meaningful gains are the objective behind what we are and what we do for your business.

  • Grow Market Share

    Whether you’re a challenger brand or an industry leader, your presence must be felt by both your customer and your competitors. Our team works to understand the best opportunities for market growth and create profitable campaigns to make it happen.

  • Increase Customer Lifetime Value

    Every project we work on is meant to be a strategic step towards our client’s long-term success. That’s why our strategy and creative aim to foster brand loyalty and ensure you’re maximizing the value of every customer.
